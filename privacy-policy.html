Overview

PV Calc is a solar performance and payback calculator for homeowners. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information the app uses, how it is used, and what we do not collect.

Information We Do Not Collect

No data collected

PV Calc does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected
  • No financial or billing information is collected
  • No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics are collected
  • No usage data or crash logs are sent to us

Location Information

Used locally, not stored

When you type a city or location into the app, that text is sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim to look up geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude). Those coordinates are then sent to the PVGIS API (EU Joint Research Centre) to retrieve local solar irradiance data.

This information is used only to perform the solar calculation you requested. It is not stored on any server we operate, not linked to your identity, and not shared with any third party beyond the APIs listed below.

The app does not use your device's GPS or request location permissions. All location input is manual and text-based.

Third-Party Services

PV Calc communicates with the following external services to perform solar calculations. These services have their own privacy policies.

PVGIS v5.2 — EU Joint Research Centre

Provides satellite-derived solar irradiance data. Receives the latitude and longitude of the location you entered. No personal data is transmitted.

re.jrc.ec.europa.eu

OpenStreetMap Nominatim

Provides free geocoding (converts a place name to coordinates). Receives the city or location name you type. No personal data is transmitted.

nominatim.openstreetmap.org

Data Storage

All inputs you enter (home profile, system preferences, cost figures) are stored only on your device using standard local app storage. This data never leaves your device except as part of the API requests described above. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

PV Calc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Advertising

PV Calc contains no advertising and no advertising SDKs. We do not use your data for targeted advertising.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date above and release an updated version of the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
